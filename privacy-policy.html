Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Fun Zone Games" mobile application ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use it. The App provides access to a collection of casual HTML5 games for entertainment purposes.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

The App does not require account registration or login. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any payment information.

2. Information Collected Automatically

  • Advertising Identifier & Device Information: Used by our advertising partners (Google AdMob, and Start.io if active) to display ads and measure performance.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous statistics such as which games are played and session duration, used to improve the App.
  • Push Notification Token: If you allow notifications, a device token (via Firebase Cloud Messaging) is used to send you updates about new games and features. You can disable this anytime in your device settings.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, which may collect information used to identify you according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob & Google Play Services
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging
  • Start.io (only if the App's ad-free option is not enabled)

4. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience, including families, but is not directed primarily at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

5. Your Choices

  • You can opt out of personalized advertising via your device's Settings > Privacy > Ads.
  • You can disable notifications via your device's App Settings.
  • You can uninstall the App at any time, which stops all data collection by it.

6. Data Security

We rely on the security measures provided by Google Play Services, Firebase, and our advertising partners. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
